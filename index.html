Zakes Mda

Author And Writer

Acclaimed South African Writer Zakes Mda on his changing South Africa.

Below is the Timeline of Zakes Mda's Life:

1948

Born in Herschel South Africa.

1969

Completed the Cambridge Overseas Certificate at Peka High School, Lesotho.

1976

Pursued his BFA (Visual Arts and Literature) at the International Academy of Arts and Literature, Zurich, Switzerland.

1984

Completed a MFA (Theater) and a MA (Mass Communication and Media) in at Ohio University, United States.

1989

Completed his PhD at the University of Cape Town, South Africa.

2011

He is a founding member and currently serves on the advisory board of African Writers Trust.

2012

Was awarded an honorary doctorate of the University of Cape Town for his contributions to world literature. His novels have been translated into 21 languages, the translation of Ways of Dying into Turkish being the latest.
